NO

chord they want.

You can get the frequencies from a tone generator online.
https://www.szynalski.com/tone-generator/

Record a couple of seconds for each chord and add them to the
program.

The chord will be played while the user is keeping the key pressed.
If the user releases the key, the chord should stop playing.
